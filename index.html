About Us

HackPrix a 36 Hours long of Hackathon

HackPrix is being organized on June 8 & 9 2024, in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, India. It's a manifestation of CodeWave Hub's commitment to empowering individuals and fostering a culture of innovation. By creating this platform, we aim to unlock the vast potential that resides within the tech enthusiasts.

At the end of this 30 hours, participants will have a chance to pitch their project and launch their ideas to an audience of peers and sponsor representatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will food & stay be provided?

We've got lots of food and snacks for everyone, stay hacky and hydrated. Rooms for resting/sleeping will be arranged at the Campus (separately for opposite genders).

Who can Participate?

We welcome undergraduates and graduate students of all majors, backgrounds, and skill levels.

Unfortunately, if you are under 18, you will not be able to attend.

Is there any Registration fee?

HackPrix is absolutely free for everyone to attend.
